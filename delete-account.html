Account Deletion

Jay Matadi Vegetable Store

We respect your privacy and provide you with the ability to delete your account at any time.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Jay Matadi Vegetable Store app.
  2. Sign in to your account.
  3. Go to Profile.
  4. Select Delete Account.
  5. Confirm your request.

If you are unable to access the app, you can also request account deletion by emailing us.

What Happens After Deletion?

  • Your account will be permanently deleted.
  • Your personal information will be removed from our active systems.
  • You will no longer be able to log in using the deleted account.

Information We May Retain

To comply with legal, tax, fraud prevention, or accounting requirements, we may retain certain order and transaction records for the period required by applicable law. This retained information is not used for marketing purposes.

Deletion Time

Account deletion requests are typically completed within 7 business days.
